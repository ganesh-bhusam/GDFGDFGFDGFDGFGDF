AdvScribbl

About

AdvScribbl is a modernized multiplayer drawing & guessing pictionary game.

Each game has a few rounds — every round a player draws their chosen word and others guess it to score points!

The player with the most points at the end is crowned the winner.

What's new

  • Premium glass-morphism UI with floating cards
  • MS Paint-style toolset (Pencil, Bucket, Line, Rect, Circle, Eraser)
  • Romanized Indian languages (Hinglish, Tamiglish, Teluglish…)
  • 🔒 Lock your username + unlock 26 premium colors for just ₹20
  • Classic 8 colored character avatars

How to play

  1. Pick a name, language, avatar. Click Play!
  2. When it's your turn — pick a word and draw it.
  3. Other players race to guess via chat.
  4. Faster guesses = more points.
  5. Most points after all rounds = winner!
