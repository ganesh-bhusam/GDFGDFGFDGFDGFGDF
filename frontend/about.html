About AdvScribbl

What is AdvScribbl?

AdvScribbl is a modernized, premium multiplayer drawing and guessing game designed for friends and communities. Gather your friends, create a private room, and take turns drawing and guessing words to score points.

How to Play

The game consists of multiple rounds. During your turn, you get to choose a word from a list of options. You have a limited time to draw that word using the MS-Paint style tools and premium color palette.

The rest of the players must guess the word as quickly as possible. The faster you guess the correct word, the more points you earn. At the end of all rounds, the player with the highest score takes the crown!

A Special Shout-out

This project was deeply inspired by the classic web game Skribbl.io. A huge shout-out to the creator of Skribbl.io for paving the way and inspiring me to build this modernized version with a beautiful UI, new brush dynamics, and extra tools. AdvScribbl stands as a tribute and a fresh take on the drawing and guessing genre!
