Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 2, 2026

1. Information We Collect

AdvScribbl is designed to be a fun, anonymous multiplayer game. We do not require you to create an account or provide any personal information to play.

When you join a game, we collect temporary, session-based data such as the nickname you choose and your connection IP address to facilitate real-time gameplay via WebSockets.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your session data is used strictly for the purpose of running the game instance. We do not track you across other websites, and we do not sell your data to third parties.

3. Local Storage

We may use local storage in your browser to save your cosmetic preferences (e.g., your chosen avatar, preferred language, or last used nickname) so you don't have to re-enter them every time you play.

4. User-Generated Content

Any drawings or chat messages you create during a game are broadcasted to the other players in your room. We do not permanently store this user-generated content on our servers once the game session concludes.

5. Contact

If you have any questions or concerns about our Privacy Policy, please visit our Contact Page.
